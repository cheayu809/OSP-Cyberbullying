How To Report Cyberbullying?

Steps to Take if You Become a Cyberbullying Victim


  • Block the bully. Do everything you can to cut off contact. De-friend him or her on social media and screen his or her calls.

  • Save screenshots of everything to use as evidence.

  • Notify social media outlets via the channel that they suggest that someone is using your name inappropriately.

  • Call for back-up. Many states now have laws anti-bullying laws, many of which cover cyberbullying. Check to see the laws in your state.

  • Make a report to Law Enforcement.

How to Report
